# KAIF Adopters

This page lists organizations and teams using KAIF in pre-production or production.

The goal is to share practical deployment patterns, interoperability feedback, and operational lessons.

## How To Add Your Organization

Open a pull request that adds one row to the table below.

Please include:
- Organization or team name
- Deployment stage
- Primary use case
- Environment
- Go-live date (or pilot start)
- Public link (optional)

## Stage Definitions

- Pilot: active evaluation, limited traffic, not business-critical.
- Pre-production: integrated into staging or shadow workloads.
- Production: serving real user or business traffic.
